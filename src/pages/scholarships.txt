John D. Chang Scholarships in Dallas-Fort Worth, Texas
The John D. Chang Scholar/Athlete Scholarship Program will honor two (2) Happy Hill Farm students at Dallas, Texas in 2006 as follows: 1. One (1) female student-athlete will be awarded a $1,500 scholarship. 2. One (1) male student-athlete will be awarded a $1,500 scholarship. Requirements: Applicants must be graduating this year and accepted by a 4 year college or University in 2006. Must be in financial need and have a minumum Grade Point Average of 3.5. Attendance in the college or University of the student's choice is required and scholarship funds will be sent directly to the school. 

FSU- John D. Chang Scholarship in Media Production
FSU- John D. Chang Scholarship in Media Production The John D.
